Grilled Chicken

Description

This is a juciy and delicious chicken recipe that wont take forever to make

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Paprika
  • MSG
  • Flour
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. First lay your chicken thighs out and poke them with a fork so they marinate quicker
  2. Add pepper, oil, MSG, and Paprika and mix
  3. Marinate for 15 mins
  4. After 15 bring your chicken out and put it in the Flour
  5. Grill skinside down for crispy chicken
  6. Plate
