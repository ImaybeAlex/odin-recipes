Chipotle Bowl

Description

This is a chipotle bowl packed with flavour and nutrients to keep you full and healthy throughout the day

This recipe is fun and easy to make!!!

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Beans
  • Chicken Al Pastore
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Lime

Steps

  1. First get put the chicken in the microwave and then put aside
  2. Then dice up your onions, and grate your cheese
  3. Cook your can of beans
  4. Slice your lime into wedges
  5. Put it all in a big bowl
